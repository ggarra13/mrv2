mrv2 v1.5.4: Professional VFX Review for Everyone
================================

I'm excited to announce the latest update to mrv2 and vmrv2 -- the open-source, high-performance professional players and review tools for the VFX, Animation and CG communities.

Whether you are working on Windows, Linux or macOS, mrv2 is designed to handle demanding workflows with ease.

Two Engines, One Goal
-------------------------

We now ship in two specialized versions to fit your hardware:

* mrv2 (OpenGL): Robust, reliable and widely compatible
* vmrv2 (Vulkan): Cutting-edge performance with support for Dynamic HDR.

Choose your Tier
------------------

While mrv2 remains open-source, we offer Donationware Licenses to unlock	professional grade features and support further development.

Pricing & Licenses
------------------

Solo ($25/yr): Essential review tools (Annotations).
Standard ($50/yr): Adds Python power for pipeline integration.
Edit ($75/yr): Adds an editing suite.
Pro ($150 - $300): Perpetual "to own" licenses with advanced voice tools and machine flexibility.
