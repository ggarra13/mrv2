mrv2 v1.4.4
=======

mrv2 and vmrv2 are open source professional players and review tools for VFX, animation and computer graphics for Windows, Linux and macOS.

It ships now in two compiled versions:
   - mrv2 with OpenGL backend  (free up to version 1.4.0 - donationware afterwards)
   - vmrv2 with Vulkan backend (donationware)

New Link Annotations to add URLs, Files or Directories annotations in the image.
Fixed and improved Saving Sequences in Vulkan baking the transform and HDR shader to OpenEXRs when Annotations is on. Improved performance of the dock panel.
Fixed resizing of OpenGL's Wayland build.  Improved performance of playback (movies and images).
